Membership Benefits:


As a valued member of Public-Library, you will enjoy the following benefits:

  • Access to a diverse collection of books across various genres.
  • Ability to borrow up to four books at a time.
  • Option to keep the books for up to One weeks (subject to renewal).
  • Access to special events, book clubs, and author meet-ups.
  • Exclusive discounts on library-sponsored programs and workshops.

Terms and Conditions:

By signing this membership form, you agree to abide by the library's rules and regulations, including but not limited to:

  • Taking responsibility for any damage or loss of borrowed books.
  • Returning borrowed books on or before the due date to avoid late fees.
  • Keeping your contact information up to date to receive library notifications.
  • Respecting other library members and staff.
  • Whenever You want to borrow a Book you have to Refill form again with Your latest Details.
